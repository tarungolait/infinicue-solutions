Our Products-Solution

Salvus SeQUr proudly emerges as the world's pioneer in the realm of cybersecurity, presenting a groundbreaking solution to combat cyber fraud. Developed and manufactured entirely in India, Salvus SeQUr has been officially recognized as a Novelty by the Indian Patent Office, a testament to its innovative prowess. Having secured a patent in February 2020 and with more pending, Salvus SeQUr stands at the forefront of cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions.


This revolutionary product addresses a wide spectrum of cyber threats, offering a comprehensive shield against skimming, cloning, shimming of credit/debit cards, SIM swap fraud, account take-over, identity theft, phishing fraud, remote access fraud, and network access control vulnerabilities. Salvus SeQUr ensures user-controlled safeguards, putting individuals in command of their personal data and thwarting potential threats effectively.


At the core of Salvus SeQUr's innovation is SeQUr AMDA (Automated Multi Device Authentication), a solution seamlessly integrated with relevant authorities to confirm the legitimate utility of personal data by the authentic user. This extra layer of security sets a new standard for user-centric protection, addressing the dynamic challenges posed by evolving cyber threats.


Salvus SeQUr stands as a beacon of commitment to advancing cybersecurity, offering a streamlined process that empowers users and secures personal data from a myriad of potential dangers. In a digital landscape fraught with risks, Salvus SeQUr stands as the vanguard, ensuring data security and instilling confidence in the face of cyber threats.
